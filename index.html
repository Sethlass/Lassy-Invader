    How to Play

    1. Use the left and right arrow keys to move your spaceship.
    2. Press the spacebar to shoot bullets at the invaders.
    3. Destroy all invaders to advance to the next level.
    4. Avoid getting hit by the invaders' bullets or letting them reach you.
    5. Your score increases with each invader you destroy.
    6. Watch out for your lives, displayed under the score!
